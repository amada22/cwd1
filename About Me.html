About Me

I'm Mohamed El Bouzari, or Hamada, from Morocco. I’m currently studying a BSc in Computer Science at Griffith College in Ireland. I have 6 months of experience in IT, specifically in web development using Nuxt.js and Laravel. I’m passionate about technology and enjoy working on coding projects. Outside of work and studies, I love staying active with sports like football, surfing, calisthenics, running, and Muay Thai.

IT Experience

I have 6 months of experience in IT, primarily focusing on web development with Nuxt.js and Laravel. During this time, I’ve developed strong skills in both front-end and back-end technologies, working on several projects that allowed me to hone my problem-solving abilities and creativity in coding.

Languages I Speak

I speak Moroccan Darija, Arabic, French, and English, with a little bit of Spanish. While I’m not fluent in all of these, my language skills have been important for my studies and in communicating with others in a diverse environment.

Hobbies

In my free time, I stay active with a variety of sports such as football, surfing, calisthenics, and running. I also practice Muay Thai to keep fit. Additionally, I’m involved in a family-run business that sells vintage jewelry, which allows me to explore my creative side.
